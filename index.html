About Aaron

Aaron hails from Atlanta, Georgia. His canon of work spans a diverse range of literary projects. His debut series, Monarch, a unique four-part exploration of an unsettled housewife's impending doomsday fears, sets the tone for his engaging storytelling style.

Aaron's subsequent works venture further into the dark corridors of psychological uncertainty. Psykosis, one such venture, weaves an intriguing narrative around a lawyer entangled in the sinister deeds of a hypnotist. Michaelmas, another of his profound narratives, transports readers to 18th-century England and encapsulates the turbulent transition from an era of magic to the advent of science.

His newest, House Fly, is a novella that chronicles the journey of a recent divorcee unknowingly stepping into a complex commitment in a new town.

Beyond the realm of books, Aaron has lent his unique voice to the spheres of film and television screenwriting. No matter the medium, his distinctive style bridges the mundane and the sublime, challenging readers and viewers alike to confront the dichotomy between light and darkness inherent in us all.

STORiCORE

Storicore is a multimedia company dedicated to creating innovative apps and original stories. We combine technology and storytelling to deliver immersive experiences across various platforms:

  • Books & E-books
  • Films & TV
  • Interactive Apps & Games

Our mission is to craft narratives that resonate with global audiences, bridging the gap between imagination and reality. Through Storicore, Aaron aims to inspire people everywhere to engage with stories that spark creativity, wonder, and reflection.
